DENTAL VACATION

We would all love to mix work and play. What if, however, you could include health and play?Dental tourism is a vacation with a twist where you can set aside the hassie of hefty dental bills in your home country and instead get affordable quality treatment somewhere else.

Hyderabad is one of India's richest cultural hubs, With a fascinating history, a distinct delectable cuisine and a thriving urban software center, this city is a bubbling pot of excitement and adventure. Hyderabad is also a leading spot for medical tourism, where people from all over the world pop in to get quality health-care at economical rates. Dental tourism today constitutes for about 30% of medical tourism in India, Owing to the low cost, skilled practitioners and flexibility in treatment, we see many patients traveling exclusively for dental health-care. At Saritha Dental Clinic we can help you save upto 80% of what you'd be spending on dental treatment back at home, be it dental implants, smile designing, cosmetic dentistry etc. We'd also help you plan your tour around India in such a manner that you receive the full benefit of your travel and dental treatment. With customized treatment plans, you could pop in for a classic hyderabadi biriyani at your leisure!
